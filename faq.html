General Questions

What is CallView?

CallView is a human-in-the-loop AI voice platform that lets your SDRs supervise and coach AI-powered sales calls in real-time. Think of it as giving your best SDR the ability to run 500+ conversations a day while maintaining full control. The AI handles the dialing and initial conversation, but humans can whisper suggestions, see live transcripts, or take over any call seamlessly.

How is CallView different from other AI voice dialers?

Most AI voice platforms are "set and forget" - you configure the AI and hope it doesn't embarrass your brand. CallView is fundamentally different:

  • Live Coaching Console: See every conversation in real-time with transcription and sentiment analysis
  • Whisper Mode: Suggest phrases to the AI mid-conversation without the prospect hearing
  • Seamless Takeover: Jump into any call seamlessly when you spot opportunity

AI & Technology

Will the AI sound robotic?

No. CallView uses voice cloning technology to replicate your top SDR's voice from just 30 seconds of audio. The AI sounds exactly like your best performer - with natural inflection, pacing, and warmth. Prospects consistently report they can't tell they're speaking with AI until we reveal it.

How does voice cloning work?

During setup, you record a 30-second sample of your top SDR speaking naturally (we provide the script). Our AI analyzes the voice characteristics - tone, pace, inflection patterns - and creates a digital voice model. This model powers all AI calls, making every conversation sound like your best rep is on the line.

What if the AI says something wrong?

This is exactly why CallView is human-in-the-loop, not autonomous. Your SDRs watch every conversation in real-time via the coaching console. If the AI goes off-script or mishandles an objection, your SDR can:

  • Whisper: Type a suggestion the AI will incorporate naturally
  • Override: Replace the AI's next response entirely
  • Takeover: Seamlessly step into the call as a human

You're always in control. The AI amplifies your team - it doesn't replace your judgment.

How does the AI handle objections?

We train the AI on your specific objection handlers using our ABQ framework (Acknowledge, Bridge, Question). During setup, we load all your common objections and responses. The AI learns how your team handles "We already have a vendor," "Not interested," "Send me an email," and dozens of other scenarios. Your SDRs can also whisper suggestions for unusual objections in real-time.

Compliance & Security

Is CallView TCPA compliant?

Yes. CallView is built for TCPA compliance from the ground up:

  • Calling Hours: Automatic enforcement of 8am-9pm local time restrictions
  • DNC Scrubbing: All lists are scrubbed against the National Do Not Call Registry before dialing
  • Human Oversight: Every call has human supervision, satisfying "reasonable efforts" requirements
  • Consent Verification: AI confirms opt-in status before proceeding with sales conversations
  • Call Recording Disclosure: Automatic disclosure where required by state law
Is my data secure?

Absolutely. CallView uses enterprise-grade security:

  • All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • SOC 2 Type II compliant infrastructure
  • Call recordings stored securely with access controls
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • GDPR-compliant data handling for international prospects

See our privacy policy for complete details.

What about industry-specific compliance (SEC, FINRA, HIPAA)?

CallView supports industry-specific compliance needs:
