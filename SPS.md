## **PART I: GOVERNANCE & ELIGIBILITY**

**Protocol Owner:** Compliance Office

* **Requirement:** Partner **MUST** possess valid DTI (Sole Proprietorship) or SEC (Corporation) registration.  
* **Prohibition:** Freelancers, "Home-Based" loose networks, and unregistered agencies are **strictly prohibited**.  
* **Tenure:** Entity must demonstrate minimum 1 Year of US B2B Campaign experience.

### **1.2 The "Zone" Audit Protocol**

* **ZONE A (Physical Audit):** Metro Manila, Central Luzon, North Luzon.  
  * *Requirement:* A CallView Tech Rep must physically visit the site to sign off on cabling and acoustics prior to "Go Live."  
  * *Provisional Clause:* Partners \>100km from HQ may launch with a Digital Audit but **MUST** pass a Physical Audit within 30 days.

* **Requirement:** The Partner must maintain **minimum One (1) Paid CallView AI Subscription Line** ($500/mo) in good standing.  
* **Activity Clause:** The Partner must log a minimum of **100 Minutes/Week** on this internal line to demonstrate active platform competency.

### **1.4 Non-Circumvention & Client Ownership**

* **The Principle:** All Clients introduced by CallView.ai remain the exclusive property of CallView.ai.  
* **Violation:** Any attempt to solicit the Client directly or bypass the Platform Fee results in immediate termination and a **Liquidated Damages Penalty of $10,000 USD** per incident.

### **1.5 IP Protection & Data Sovereignty**

* **Trade Secrets:** All CallView SOPs, Training Manuals, AI Prompts, and Scripts are Trade Secrets.  
* **Data Sovereignty:** All Data (Leads, Recordings, Transcripts, Enriched Notes) provided by or generated on the Platform is the **Exclusive Property of CallView.ai**.  
* **Destruction:** Upon termination, Partner must certify the destruction of all local data copies. Usage of CallView data for other clients is Theft.

### **1.6 Non-Compete & Non-Replication Covenant**

* **Restriction:** During the term of this agreement and for a period of **24 Months** post-termination, the Service Partner agrees NOT to develop, market, or invest in any competing **"AI Voice Orchestration"** software platform globally.

### **1.7 Brand Usage & Identity**

* **Designation:** You are an **"Authorized Service Partner."** You are NOT "CallView Corporate."  
* **Prohibition:** Partners may not use "CallView" in their URL/Domain Name or issue press releases without written approval from the CRO.

### **1.8 Prohibition on Subcontracting**

* **Direct Labor Only:** All services must be performed by **direct employees** of the Partner entity.  
* **Restriction:** Outsourcing, subcontracting, or farming out seats to third-party freelancers or "home-based" networks is strictly prohibited and constitutes a breach of security.

## **PART II: INFRASTRUCTURE & "IRON DOME" TECH**

**Protocol Owner:** IT Director

### **2.1 The CallView Provisioned Stack ("Business-in-a-Box")**

We provide the technology; you provide the talent. All Authorized Partners receive full access to:

* **The CRM Core:** Enterprise License for the **GoHighLevel (GHL) System**, fully integrated with the CallView Operational Dialer.  
* **Data Intelligence:** High-intent, verified **Lead Lists** are pre-loaded directly into the GHL Campaign logic.  
* **Command Center:** Access to the **Service Partner Global Dashboard** for real-time fleet monitoring.  
* **Operational Support:** Assignment of a **Dedicated Service Partner Manager**.  
* **Restriction:** This stack is for **CallView Client Fulfillment ONLY**. Using these licenses for non-CallView campaigns results in immediate chargebacks and license revocation.

### **2.2 Partner Hardware Obligations**

Any station failing these specs will be blocked from the Dashboard.

* **Processor:** Intel Core i5 (8th Gen+) or AMD Ryzen 5 Equivalent.  
* **RAM:** **16GB Minimum.** (Non-negotiable).  
* **Audio:** **Active Noise Cancellation (ANC)** Headsets (Jabra/Poly) Required. USB Connection Only.

### **2.3 Network Topology**

* **Connectivity:** **Hard-Wired Ethernet (LAN) ONLY.** (Wi-Fi is prohibited).  
* **Bandwidth:** Dedicated 25 Mbps Download / 10 Mbps Upload **per agent**.  
* **Latency & Jitter:** Ping to US-West \< 200ms; Jitter \< 30ms.

### **2.4 Technical Support**

**ALL** Partner technical issues must be routed through the **Dedicated Trouble Ticket System** embedded in the main chat widget.

* **Escalation Layer:** Critical outages may be escalated via the **Official Partner Discord Server** for real-time engineering support.  
* **Failover:** Tickets may be routed to partners@callview.ai if the widget is down.

### **2.5 Business Continuity (BCP)**

* **Force Majeure:** In the event of natural disaster (Typhoon), Partners are granted a **24-Hour Recovery Window** before seats are temporarily migrated to protect Client campaigns.

## **PART VI: SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA)**

### **6.1 The Partner’s Obligation: "The 50-70 Rule"**

The Service Partner guarantees the delivery of **Verified Sales Appointments (VSAs)** based on a Standard Deployment Pod (5 Agents).

* **Minimum Threshold:** **50 VSAs / Month**.  
* **Target Zone:** **70+ VSAs / Month**.

### **6.2 The Principal’s Obligation: "The Revenue Partnership"**

CallView.ai commits to maintaining a **Target Close Rate of 30%** on all Qualified Held Appointments.

* **The "Bad Client" Protection Clause:** If a Client closes **\< 20%** of the Partner's *Verified* Appointments over a 60-day period, CallView reserves the right to **replace the Client** to protect the Partner's seat revenue.

### **6.3 Definitions of Quality (SQL Criteria)**

An appointment is ONLY **valid towards KPI** (Qualified) if it meets the **SQL Criteria (BANT)**:

1. **ICP Match:** US B2B, \>$1M Revenue.  
2. **Decision Maker:** C-Level, VP, or Director.  
3. **Timeline:** Prospect interest within **\< 90 Days**.  
4. **Show Rate:** Prospect attends the Zoom call.  
5. **Intent:** Recording proves the prospect knows this is a Sales Meeting.

### **6.4 Remediation**

* **Validation Window:** CallView Ops has **48 Business Hours** to validate appointments.  
* **PIP:** Delivery of \<50 Appointments triggers a Performance Improvement Plan. Continued failure converts retainer to Commission Only.
